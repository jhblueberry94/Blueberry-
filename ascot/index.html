Ascot, Berkshire

A racecourse economy with a private client advisory core

Ascot's economy is defined in large part by Ascot Racecourse, which supports an estimated £264.5 million in Gross Value Added and around 3,903 full-time equivalent jobs across its operations, supply chain, and visitor spending, with Royal Ascot alone supporting close to 1,930 of those jobs. Around that racing calendar sits a smaller, quieter economy: a genuine cluster of private wealth management and financial advisory firms, including several FCA-regulated practices based in and around the village, serving high-net-worth private clients.

These are two very different commercial worlds sharing the same postcode. Corporate hospitality and event businesses build their calendar around racedays. Private client advisers build their reputation over years, largely through referral, and rarely show up on LinkedIn in a way that reflects the trust their clients place in them. In both cases, buyers do their homework before making contact, and a credible, consistent LinkedIn presence is what gets a founder or adviser noticed during that research.

Blueberry Media is based in Hungerford, Berkshire, within the same Thames Valley business community. We understand the specific demands of building LinkedIn credibility for private client and advisory businesses, where tone and trust matter more than volume.

Who we work with in Ascot

Our Ascot-area clients tend to be founders and partners at private wealth management, financial advisory, and estate or tax planning practices, serving private clients who expect discretion and expertise in equal measure. We also work with founders in event hospitality, corporate services, and specialist suppliers connected to the racing calendar.

Many are excellent practitioners whose growth has come almost entirely through referral and reputation. They are naturally cautious about anything that looks like broadcast marketing, which is exactly why a genuine, expertise-led LinkedIn presence works well here. It extends an existing reputation to new buyers without asking anyone to compromise on tone or professionalism.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you a set of questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is delivered entirely remotely. You do not need to travel to a meeting or adjust your schedule beyond the fortnightly recording call. Everything else is handled.
