Slough, Berkshire

Europe's largest trading estate, and why LinkedIn matters here

Slough Trading Estate is the largest and oldest continuously operating privately owned trading estate in Europe, spanning around 486 acres and more than 600 buildings. It provides a base for over 500 businesses and a working population of roughly 20,000 people, including global names such as Mars, DHL, Ferrari, and O2. Slough's proximity to Heathrow, around 20 minutes by car, adds a logistics and aviation-support dimension that few other Berkshire towns can match.

That scale of enterprise and logistics activity creates a specific commercial character. Slough has been described as Britain's second startup capital after London, and the businesses that supply, service, and sell into the estate's larger tenants tend to be highly commercial and fast-moving. For founders and consultants operating in this environment, LinkedIn is where procurement teams, operations leads, and senior buyers from those larger organisations do their research before any formal process begins.

Blueberry Media is based in Hungerford, Berkshire, within the same Thames Valley business community. We understand the enterprise and logistics character of Slough's economy and what buyers embedded in that environment are actually looking for when they research suppliers on LinkedIn.

Who we work with in Slough

Our Slough-area clients tend to run or lead B2B businesses that sell into the enterprise, logistics, and industrial operations concentrated on and around the trading estate. Common profiles include specialist logistics and supply chain consultants, industrial services providers, technology and IT infrastructure specialists, and business advisory professionals.

Many are founders who have built strong commercial relationships through direct account management and tendering but have no LinkedIn presence to show for their expertise. Given the scale and international reach of Slough's largest employers, a consistent LinkedIn presence is a direct way to be visible to procurement and operations buyers researching new suppliers, wherever in the world those buyers happen to be based.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you a set of questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is delivered entirely remotely. You do not need to travel to a meeting or adjust your schedule beyond the fortnightly recording call. Everything else is handled.
