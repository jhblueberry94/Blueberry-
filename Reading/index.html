Reading, Berkshire

One of the UK's most significant regional tech hubs and why LinkedIn matters here

Reading consistently ranks as the UK's most significant technology and business hub outside London. Oracle, Microsoft, Huawei, and hundreds of scale-up and professional services businesses operate from Thames Valley Park, Green Park, and the town centre. The concentration of commercially active organisations is matched by a professional community that uses LinkedIn as its primary business network.

For B2B founders and consultants operating in Reading, that creates both an opportunity and a challenge. The opportunity: a consistent, expert LinkedIn presence puts you in front of buyers and decision-makers from the region's largest employers during the research phase of their buying process. The challenge: most Reading-based founders are not showing up on LinkedIn in any meaningful way, which means the field is largely open for those who are.

Blueberry Media is based just 35 miles from Reading in Hungerford, Berkshire. We understand the Thames Valley business community, the kinds of B2B conversations happening in the region, and what decision-makers in the area are actually looking for when they research providers on LinkedIn.

Who we work with in Reading

Our Reading-area clients tend to run or lead B2B businesses. Many are consultants, technology founders, or professional services leaders who have built strong reputations through their work but have not found a way to reflect that credibility on LinkedIn consistently. A significant number operate from Reading's key business parks and serve clients across the UK, where LinkedIn is their primary channel for reaching new buyers.

Common sectors include technology consulting, IT infrastructure, financial services advisory, management consulting, engineering, and specialist B2B services. Reading's position as a tech hub means we also work with SaaS founders and technology executives who want to build profile in a market that reads LinkedIn as a signal of credibility before any other conversation happens.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you a set of questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is delivered entirely remotely. You do not need to travel to a meeting or adjust your schedule beyond the fortnightly recording call. Everything else is handled.

Frequently asked questions

Does Blueberry Media work with businesses based in Reading? +

Yes. We work with B2B founders and executives based in Reading and across the Thames Valley. The service is delivered entirely remotely, so location makes no difference to how we work or the results we achieve. Reading-based clients work with us in exactly the same way as clients anywhere in the UK or internationally.

Why is LinkedIn particularly important for Reading-based B2B founders? +

Reading is one of the UK's most significant regional technology hubs outside London, with Oracle, Microsoft, Huawei, and hundreds of scale-up and enterprise businesses operating in the area. That concentration of commercially active organisations makes LinkedIn a high-value channel: buyers and decision-makers from those businesses use it actively, and a consistent founder presence puts you in front of them during the research phase of their buying process.

What types of Reading businesses do you work with? +

We work with B2B founders, consultants, and senior executives across technology, professional services, financial services, engineering, and business advisory. Reading's tech-heavy business community means many of our enquiries come from founders at SaaS companies, IT consultancies, and specialist advisory businesses operating from Thames Valley Park, Green Park, and the town centre.

How does the service work? +

We start with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. You receive questions in advance, and you talk naturally about your market, your thinking, and your experience. From that conversation, we produce three to four LinkedIn posts per week that sound like you, because they come directly from you. Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy aligned to your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report.

Is there a long-term contract? +

No. All packages are monthly rolling with 30 days notice. No setup fees, no exit penalties, no minimum term.

How is Blueberry Media different from a Reading-based marketing agency? +

We do one thing: done-for-you LinkedIn thought leadership for B2B founders and executives. We are not a full-service marketing agency. The entire service is built around a fortnightly conversation with you, producing content that reflects your genuine expertise and voice rather than generic marketing copy. That specificity is what makes the difference on LinkedIn.
