Newbury, Berkshire

Where Blueberry Media started, and why that matters

Blueberry Media is based in Hungerford, seven miles south-west of Newbury. This is where the business started, and the Newbury and west Berkshire business community is one we have been part of since the beginning. Our first clients were B2B founders and executives based in this area, and that local understanding informs how we approach the market.

Newbury itself has a distinct commercial character. Vodafone's UK headquarters sits at Vodafone House on the A4, employing thousands of people and anchoring a significant technology and professional services ecosystem around the town. Greenham Business Park hosts a range of specialist B2B businesses. The town centre and surrounding villages are home to consultants, advisors, and independent professional services firms whose founders have built strong reputations through their work rather than through a visible LinkedIn presence.

That is the pattern we see consistently in the Newbury area. Excellent work, genuine expertise, strong referral networks, and a LinkedIn profile that does not reflect any of it. Blueberry Media exists to close that gap.

Who we work with in Newbury

Our Newbury-area clients include consultants, technology specialists, financial advisors, management consultants, and professional services leaders. Many are sole founders or partners at small to mid-sized firms who have built their businesses through referrals and relationships but want to create a sustainable channel for inbound enquiries that does not depend on who they happen to know.

We also work with executives at larger organisations in the Newbury area who want to build professional visibility on LinkedIn, whether to support business development, to attract talent, or to establish a point of view in their market. The service adapts to both profiles without changing its core approach.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is fully remote. There are no in-person requirements, no travel, and no disruption to your working week beyond the fortnightly recording call.

Is Blueberry Media actually based near Newbury? +

Why is LinkedIn relevant for Newbury-based B2B founders? +

Newbury is home to Vodafone's UK headquarters at Vodafone House on the A4, Greenham Business Park, and a wide range of professional services, technology, and specialist B2B businesses. Many Newbury-area founders sell to national and international clients who research providers on LinkedIn before any other contact.
