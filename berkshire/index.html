Based in Berkshire

A LinkedIn content agency rooted in the Thames Valley.

Blueberry Media is based in Hungerford, Berkshire, working with B2B founders and senior leaders across the region and nationally. Our clients include consultants, technology founders, professional services leaders, and executives at growth-stage businesses across Berkshire, Oxfordshire, Wiltshire, Hampshire, and the broader UK.

While our work is entirely remote, we're proud to be rooted in the Berkshire business community. The Thames Valley corridor is home to a significant concentration of B2B businesses, technology companies, and professional services firms whose leaders are exactly the kind of people we work best with: experienced, credible, and ready to build a LinkedIn presence that matches the quality of what they do.

What we do for Berkshire-based founders and leaders

We provide done-for-you LinkedIn thought leadership for B2B founders, consultants, and executives. The service is built around a simple premise: you have valuable expertise and a genuine point of view, but you don't have the time or system to share it consistently. We fix that.

The process starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that sound exactly like you, because they come directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and any inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and video clip production.

Why LinkedIn matters for Berkshire B2B businesses

The B2B landscape in Berkshire and the Thames Valley is competitive. Many founders and consultants in the region are excellent at what they do, but rely almost entirely on referrals and existing relationships for new business. LinkedIn changes the surface area of that opportunity.

Research from 6sense shows that B2B buyers complete over 60% of their decision-making process before ever contacting a supplier. They're researching online, reading content, and forming views about who to shortlist before they pick up the phone. A consistent, credible LinkedIn presence puts you in that research phase rather than only in the conversation that follows it.

For businesses in Berkshire targeting clients across the UK or internationally, LinkedIn is one of the few channels that builds genuine authority at scale, without the costs of traditional advertising or event sponsorship. The investment is time, and that's exactly what we remove from the equation.
