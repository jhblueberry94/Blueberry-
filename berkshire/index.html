The opportunity

Executive visibility on LinkedIn: the US/UK gap

American B2B founders and executives post on LinkedIn at significantly higher rates than their UK counterparts. This is not a matter of having more to say. It is a cultural default: US business culture normalises public professional presence as part of how founders build credibility and attract commercial opportunity.

In the UK, and particularly outside London, the inverse tendency is common. Many Berkshire and Thames Valley founders view consistent LinkedIn posting as self-promotional, unnecessary, or something they will get to eventually. That restraint has a cost. When buyers are researching providers, the founders who appear in their feeds during that research phase are the ones who earn consideration first.

The gap is an opportunity. A Berkshire-based founder who posts consistently and with genuine authority on their area of expertise does not need London visibility to win UK-wide work. LinkedIn is not location-dependent. The field is largely open because most regional B2B leaders are not on it in any meaningful way. That is precisely the window Blueberry Media is built to help founders use.

What we do for Berkshire-based founders and leaders

We provide done-for-you LinkedIn thought leadership for B2B founders, consultants, and executives. The service is built around a simple premise: you have valuable expertise and a genuine point of view, but you do not have the time or system to share it consistently. We fix that.

The process starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that sound exactly like you, because they come directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and any inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and video clip production.

Why LinkedIn matters for Berkshire B2B businesses

The B2B landscape in Berkshire and the Thames Valley is competitive. Many founders and consultants in the region are excellent at what they do, but rely almost entirely on referrals and existing relationships for new business. LinkedIn changes the surface area of that opportunity.

Research from 6sense shows that B2B buyers complete over 60% of their decision-making process before ever contacting a supplier. They are researching online, reading content, and forming views about who to shortlist before they pick up the phone. A consistent, credible LinkedIn presence puts you in that research phase rather than only in the conversation that follows it.

For businesses in Berkshire targeting clients across the UK or internationally, LinkedIn is one of the few channels that builds genuine authority at scale, without the costs of traditional advertising or event sponsorship. The investment is time, and that is exactly what we remove from the equation.
