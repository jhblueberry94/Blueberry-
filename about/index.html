About us

We believe the best content comes from real conversations.

Blueberry Media is a small, focused team based in Berkshire. We work directly with founders and senior leaders who want to build genuine authority on LinkedIn.

Our story

Most content agencies treat LinkedIn like a broadcast channel. Push posts out with business updates and hope people listen.

We built Blueberry Media from a different starting point. Ten years working with leaders across boutique consultancies and enterprise organisations, learning how people form trust, build credibility, and decide who to follow and who to buy from. People have always bought from people. That's more true now than ever.

Audiences want to learn about the person behind the business. Your story, your thinking, your perspective. People and stories drive connection. Brands don't.

That is the insight Blueberry Media is built on. We take what is already in your head, formed by your experience, your frameworks, your instincts, your opinions, and turn it into content that builds the kind of familiarity and trust that shortens every sales conversation and makes people understand you.

What we believe

Leaders should be visible

The most successful companies are led by visible, trusted leaders. Your voice is a competitive advantage.

Authenticity beats performance

Real expertise and genuine perspective will always outperform manufactured content. We help you share yours.

Consistency compounds

Authority isn't built overnight. Strategic, consistent content creates compounding returns over time.

Build your unfair advantage

You are making sales harder if nobody knows you exist. Attract warmer conversations from people who already understand what you do.
