About us

We believe the best content comes from real conversations.

Blueberry Media is a small, focused team based in Berkshire. We work directly with founders and senior leaders who want to build genuine authority on LinkedIn.

Our story

Blueberry Media started from a simple observation: the founders and leaders with the most valuable insight to share were the ones with the least time to share it. They knew LinkedIn mattered. They had plenty to say. But between running a business and everything that comes with it, content was always the thing that got pushed back.

We built a process that removes that friction entirely. A short recorded conversation every two weeks is all we need. Everything else, the writing, the scheduling, the reporting, is handled by us. The result is a consistent LinkedIn presence that sounds authentically like the leader, because it comes directly from them.

We work with a deliberately small number of clients so every relationship gets proper attention. Josh Huggins leads every account personally. There are no account managers standing between you and the work.

How we think

Three principles behind everything we do.

Authenticity over polish

The most effective LinkedIn content comes from genuine thinking, specific experience, and a real point of view. We never write from a template or produce content that could belong to anyone.

Commercial focus

Visibility for its own sake doesn't interest us. Every piece of content is written with a commercial outcome in mind: warmer conversations, faster trust, and a pipeline that moves.

Fewer clients, better work

We limit the number of clients we work with so we can give each one proper time and attention. You won't be handed off to a junior writer or left waiting for a response.
