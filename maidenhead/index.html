Maidenhead, Berkshire

A global technology base on the Thames, and the LinkedIn opportunity

Maidenhead is home to Adobe's EMEA headquarters, a prominent Grade A office occupying a central position in the town since 2011. Alongside Adobe, the wider Maidenhead area hosts technology, engineering, and financial services employers, reflecting a town whose economy runs on more than its riverside setting. As part of the Royal Borough of Windsor and Maidenhead, and with the Elizabeth Line and Great Western Railway putting central London under 30 minutes away, Maidenhead attracts a genuinely internationally-connected professional community.

That combination of global technology presence and fast rail access to London means Maidenhead-based B2B founders are often competing for attention alongside far larger, better-resourced organisations. LinkedIn is the most direct way to close that gap: a consistent, credible presence puts an independent founder or consultant in front of the same buyers and decision-makers who are already researching the bigger names on the platform.

Blueberry Media is based in Hungerford, Berkshire, within the same Thames Valley business community. We understand the region's mix of global technology operations and independent professional services firms, and what decision-makers are actually looking for when they research providers on LinkedIn.

Who we work with in Maidenhead

Our Maidenhead-area clients are typically founders, consultants, or senior executives at B2B technology, engineering, and financial services businesses, alongside independent professional services firms operating from the town centre and surrounding business parks.

Common sectors include technology consulting, engineering services, financial and business advisory, and specialist B2B services. Many have strong existing client relationships but no consistent way to reach buyers outside them. LinkedIn, built from genuine expertise and delivered consistently, is what extends that reputation to a wider commercial audience in a town where much larger technology operations already set the bar for visibility.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you a set of questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is delivered entirely remotely. You do not need to travel to a meeting or adjust your schedule beyond the fortnightly recording call. Everything else is handled.

Maidenhead is home to Adobe's EMEA headquarters, alongside a wide range of technology, engineering, and financial services businesses.
