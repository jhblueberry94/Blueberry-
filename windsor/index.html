Windsor, Berkshire

A royal address, a professional-services base, and a LinkedIn gap

Windsor's economy runs on more than tourism. Behind the castle and the high street sits a genuine cluster of consultancies, advisory firms, and specialist B2B businesses operating from offices around St Leonards Road and the wider town centre, many serving clients well beyond Berkshire. As part of the Royal Borough of Windsor and Maidenhead, and with Heathrow a short drive away, a number of Windsor-based firms operate with an internationally-facing client base.

These are founders and senior partners with real, differentiated expertise, operating in a location that carries its own reputation. Few of them have built a LinkedIn presence that reflects either. For a Windsor-based B2B business, LinkedIn is the most direct way to put that expertise in front of buyers who are researching suppliers long before they make contact.

Blueberry Media is based in Hungerford, Berkshire, within the same Thames Valley business community. We understand the region's professional services landscape and what decision-makers are actually looking for when they research providers on LinkedIn.

Who we work with in Windsor

Our Windsor-area clients are typically founders, consultants, or senior partners at B2B professional and advisory firms, alongside executives at scaling businesses who need to build authority independent of their company brand.

Common sectors include professional and financial advisory, private-client and wealth services, specialist consultancy, and technology. Many have strong local and referral networks but no consistent way to reach buyers outside them. LinkedIn, built from genuine expertise and delivered consistently, is what extends that reputation to a wider commercial audience.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you a set of questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is delivered entirely remotely. You do not need to travel to a meeting or adjust your schedule beyond the fortnightly recording call. Everything else is handled.
