Wokingham, Berkshire

Professional services at scale, and the LinkedIn gap

Wokingham borough has one of the highest concentrations of professional services businesses in Berkshire. It sits at the eastern edge of the Thames Valley corridor, adjacent to Reading's technology cluster and well-connected to London, making it a natural home for consultants, advisors, and specialist B2B firms who serve a national client base.

Winnersh Triangle is the area's most prominent business park, hosting a range of technology and professional services operations alongside Reading's Green Park to the west. The broader borough includes a significant number of home-based and small-office B2B businesses whose founders have built strong local reputations and referral networks but have not yet established a LinkedIn presence that reflects their expertise to buyers outside those networks.

That is the gap we work with. Wokingham-area founders tend to be excellent at their work and connected within their existing client community. The question is how to extend that credibility to buyers who have not yet met them. LinkedIn, done consistently and with genuine substance, is the most direct answer to that question for B2B professional services.

Who we work with in Wokingham

Our Wokingham-area clients are typically founders or senior partners at B2B professional services firms. Common profiles include management consultants, financial advisors, HR specialists, IT consultants, business coaches, and specialist service providers across legal, engineering, and business advisory.

Many are at a scaling stage where the referral model that brought early growth is no longer sufficient to sustain the next level. They want an inbound channel that does not depend on who they happen to know. LinkedIn, built around genuine expertise and a consistent presence, is the most reliable way to create that channel for B2B professional services businesses.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is fully remote. No in-person meetings, no travel, no disruption to your working week beyond the fortnightly recording call.

Wokingham has one of the highest concentrations of professional services businesses in Berkshire, with a significant cluster of consultants, advisors, and specialist B2B firms operating from Winnersh Triangle and the wider borough.
