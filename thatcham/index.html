Thatcham, Berkshire

An engineering and automotive research base, and the LinkedIn gap

Thatcham is best known in B2B circles as the home of Thatcham Research, the insurer-funded organisation based at Colthrop Way that sets vehicle safety and security standards used across the UK motor industry. Around it sits Colthrop Business Park and a wider base of engineering and manufacturing businesses, giving the town a genuinely technical, industrial character that sits alongside its more visible identity as a Kennet Valley market town on the Kennet and Avon Canal.

That combination of specialist research, engineering, and manufacturing means Thatcham-based B2B founders are often selling into a technically literate buyer base: insurers, automotive suppliers, engineering firms, and manufacturers who expect genuine expertise rather than generic marketing. LinkedIn is where those buyers do their research before making contact, and a credible, consistent presence is what makes an independent founder or specialist consultancy visible during that phase.

Blueberry Media is based a short distance away in Hungerford, Berkshire, within the same west Berkshire business community. We understand the technical, engineering-led character of Thatcham's economy and what buyers in that world are actually looking for when they research suppliers on LinkedIn.

Who we work with in Thatcham

Our Thatcham-area clients tend to be founders, consultants, or senior specialists in engineering, manufacturing, automotive and insurance-related services, and technical business advisory. Several operate businesses that sell into or alongside the research, testing, and standards work associated with the town.

Many have built genuine technical authority in their field through years of hands-on work, but that authority rarely shows up anywhere buyers can find it outside their existing network. LinkedIn, built from real expertise and delivered consistently, is the most direct way to put that authority in front of new buyers in engineering and automotive-adjacent markets.

How the service works

Every engagement starts with a 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send you a set of questions in advance. You talk naturally about your market, your experience, and your current thinking. From that conversation, we build three to four LinkedIn posts per week that reflect your voice accurately, because the material comes directly from you.

Every package includes profile optimisation, a content strategy built around your commercial goals, and a monthly performance report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and inbound signals. Higher-tier packages add newsletter writing, outreach management, and short-form video production from your recorded conversation.

The service is delivered entirely remotely. You do not need to travel to a meeting or adjust your schedule beyond the fortnightly recording call. Everything else is handled.
