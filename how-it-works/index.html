How it works

A simple process built around your time.

One conversation every two weeks. We handle everything else. Here's exactly what that looks like from day one.

Most clients are live with content within 10 days of their first call. The average time commitment is under two hours per month.

01 Strategy & ICP Alignment
02 The Content Interview
03 Create & Refine
04 Distribute & Build Visibility
01

Weeks 1 to 2

Strategy & ICP Alignment

The foundation everything else is built on. We define exactly who you are talking to, what you stand for, and how your content should feel, before a single post goes live.

What's included

  • Business and audience analysis
  • Ideal Client Profile mapping
  • Narrative and messaging framework
  • Content territory definition
  • Channel and cadence planning
02

Every 2 weeks

The Content Interview

A structured 45-minute recorded conversation every two weeks. We send questions in advance. You talk naturally. We record, transcribe, and extract everything.

What's included

  • Questions sent 48 hours in advance
  • No scripting, no prep required
  • Full recording and transcript
  • Content extraction and ideation
  • Asset planning for the next two weeks
03

Within 14 days

Create & Refine

We edit, write, and polish everything in your voice, not ours. Posts, video clips, articles, newsletter copy, all produced from a single conversation.

What's included

  • LinkedIn posts 3 to 4 per week
  • Short-form video clips
  • Newsletter or article content
  • Your review and approval
  • Unlimited refinements to voice
04

Ongoing, every month

Distribute & Build Visibility

We publish at optimal times, manage connection outreach to your ICP, track performance, and send a monthly report. You stay visible without lifting a finger.

What's included

  • Scheduled publishing and optimisation
  • ICP connection outreach
  • Engagement monitoring
  • Monthly performance report
  • Strategy refinement based on data
FAQ

About the process.

The first session is a 90-minute onboarding conversation. We cover your goals, your audience, your content pillars, and what you want LinkedIn to do commercially. You leave knowing exactly what we're building and why.

After onboarding, the ongoing commitment is one 45-minute Content Call every two weeks. We send questions in advance so there is no prep required. Everything else we handle.

First content is live within 10 days of your onboarding call. From that point posts go out consistently every week.

You approve everything before it goes live. If something does not sound right we refine it until it does. Your voice is the whole point.

Your market is researching your point of view. Make sure they're the right ones.
