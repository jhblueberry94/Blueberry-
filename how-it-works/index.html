How it works

A simple process built around your time.

One conversation every two weeks. We handle everything else. Here's exactly what that looks like from day one.

Most clients are live with content within 10 days of their first call. The average time commitment is under two hours per month.

01

Strategy and onboarding

We start with a 90-minute onboarding session covering your goals, your audience, and your three content pillars. We build your initial content framework and give you a clear picture of what the first 90 days will look like. You leave knowing exactly what we're building and why.

Time required from you: 90 minutes, once.

1
Goals review

We map your commercial objectives to a content strategy. Pipeline, hiring, partnerships, or reputation: each requires a different mix.

2
Audience and ICP definition

We define who you're talking to so every post is written with the right reader in mind.

3
Three content pillars

Market, Service, Personal: your framework for showing up consistently without running out of things to say.

02

The content call

Every two weeks, a 45-minute recorded conversation. We send you three to five questions in advance so you know the territory. On the call, we explore your thinking on a theme, a market shift, a recent experience, or a point of view. It's a natural conversation, not an interview.

Time required from you: 45 minutes, every two weeks.

Example question types

"What's one thing your best clients understand about [your market] that most people get wrong?"
"What did you learn from a recent client engagement that surprised you?"
"Where do you think your industry is heading in the next two years, and why?"
03

Content production and approval

We take the recording and extract everything useful. Your language, your thinking, your specific examples. We shape it into posts that sound exactly like you, because they come directly from you. You receive a draft schedule for review, with the option to approve, adjust, or flag anything.

Time required from you: 10 to 20 minutes per review cycle.

What we produce per call

6 to 8 LinkedIn posts
One long-form carousel or document post (Growth+)
Newsletter draft (Growth+)
Video clip scripts (Authority)
04

Publishing, outreach, and reporting

We publish your content on schedule, monitor engagement, and manage any outreach programme included in your package. Every month, you receive a clear report covering impressions, profile views, connection growth, and any inbound activity linked to your content. If something needs adjusting, we tell you and we fix it.

Time required from you: zero. We handle this entirely.

Monthly report covers

Total impressions and reach
Engagement rate by post type
Profile view growth
New ICP connections added
Inbound enquiries and signals
